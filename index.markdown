Hi! I'm Maharaj, a third-year Computer Science Ph.D. student at the [Natural Language and Information Processing (NLIP) Lab](https://nlip-lab.github.io/nlip) in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at the [Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad (IITH)](https://iith.ac.in/) supervised by [Prof. Maunendra Sankar Desarkar](https://people.iith.ac.in/maunendra/index.html) and [Dr. Anoop Kunchukuttan](http://anoopk.in/).
